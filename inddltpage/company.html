about us

Established in 2017 and certified by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India on 10 June 2020, DOLITTLE TECHNOLOGY PVT LTD is a full-stack Product Engineering and Development Company with its development centre in Hyderabad, Telangana, and Support/Operation centre in Pune, Maharashtra. We are a highly experienced offshore outsourcing, web development and consulting services, that provide comprehensive IT services to cater to all the needs of IT consulting and software development in diverse organizations. As part of business expansion, DLT has now delved into new domains including hardware-based and service-based programs. We develop customized software/applications as per customers’ requirements to gratify their needs. We convey the best services to our clients with the help of our supportive and highly skilled team. Our team is highly experienced & professional, dedicated to providing complete IT solutions to our clients under one roof. Packed with the latest technology and knowledge-ware, we provide user-friendly and customized solutions to our clients globally. Our association is renowned to provide timely, reliable, implementable and user-friendly software solutions blended with appropriate technologies for fulfilling customers’ requirements.
